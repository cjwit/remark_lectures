What strategies could be useful? --- # Kora .image-float-right.image-40[![Griot]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/kora.png)] * Construction * 21- or 22-stringed harp * Leather straps to tune * Made from a blessed wood, calabash, leather, and fishing line * Musical elements * Bass * Accompaniment/melody * Improvisation * Artists * Toumani Diabaté * Mamadou Diabaté * Bubakar Cissokho --- class: middle, center ### Ledye Diabate: “Kaira” demonstration --- class: middle, center ### Toumani Diabate: “Cantelowes” --- # Balafon .image-float-right.image-40[![Griot]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/balafon.png)] * Construction * Graduated wooden bars * Gourd resonators * Spider egg... * Rubber ended mallets * Used to have equidistant notes --- class: middle, center ### Adama Diabaté: Balafon demonstration --- # Ngoni (Koni) .image-float-right.image-40[![Griot]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/ngoni.png)] * Construction * Hollow, wooden body * Single long neck * Stretched hide for membrane * 4 to 7 strings (usually 4), some shorter than others * Various sizes, shapes across WA * Artists * Bassekou Kouyaté * Used to be the central Mande instrument, now dominated by the kora and guitar --- class: middle, center ### Bassekou Koyaté and Ngoni Ba --- # Guitar .image-float-right.image-30[![Griot]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/dakar-guitar.png)] * Tradition?
